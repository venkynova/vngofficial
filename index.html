THE BIG PICTURE

NOVA GENERATION VISION

VNG isn't just games or websites — it's a movement. A generation of creators, players and builders who make things that matter.

💡
WHAT IS THE NOVA GENERATION?
The Venky Nova Generation is a community-first creator movement built around three pillars: Build, Play, and Inspire. We create games, tools and content that prove what one passionate person with a vision can build — and invite others to join the journey and build their own.
🎯
WHY VNG EXISTS
Most indie developers build in silence. VNG exists to build in public — showing every step, every mistake, every breakthrough. We believe that transparency inspires the next generation of creators who think "if they can do it, so can I." That's the Nova effect.
🚀
FUTURE GOALS
Launch a commercially successful game before 2028. Build a VNG creator network of 50+ indie devs. Host our first live tournament with a ₹5L+ prize pool. Establish VNG as a recognised indie studio from India on the global stage.
